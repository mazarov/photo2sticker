# Design Brief (Technical Specification)  
## Landing Page for **Lovable** (Telegram-first AI Service)

**Role context:**  
Senior Product Designer · UX/UI Lead · Conversion Optimizer

---

## 1. Project Context

**Product:** Lovable  
**Type:** AI-powered AI-to-sticker service via Telegram bot  
**Platform:** Web landing page (mobile-first, desktop secondary)

**Key constraints:**
- No input fields or forms
- No text typing on the landing page
- Single primary user action
- Primary goal: **open Lovable in Telegram**

---

## 2. Landing Page Goal

Convert visitors into Telegram users by guiding them to click  
**“Open in Telegram”** with minimal friction and maximum emotional clarity.

The landing page must:
- Explain the value in seconds
- Visually show the transformation
- Feel fun, simple, and effortless
- Eliminate any perceived complexity

---

## 3. Visual Style

### 3.1 Emotional Tone
- Friendly
- Playful
- Creative
- Lighthearted
- “Instant magic” feeling

### 3.2 Design Direction
- Consumer entertainment product
- Sticker-first, result-first visuals
- Dark UI + bright expressive characters
- Telegram-native aesthetics

### 3.3 What to Avoid
- Corporate AI visuals
- Technical UI metaphors
- Complex layouts or explanations
- Any element that looks like setup or configuration

---

## 4. Color Palette

### Background
- Dark navy / deep blue / near-black
- Optional subtle pattern (icons, doodles, outlines)

### Primary Accent
- Purple → pink gradient
- Used for:
  - CTA buttons
  - Key words in headlines
  - Directional arrows and highlights

### Secondary Colors
- Cyan / light blue for steps and secondary accents
- White for text
- Soft glow or shadow effects allowed (subtle)

---

## 5. Typography

### Font Style
- Modern rounded sans-serif
- Friendly, non-corporate
- High readability on mobile

### Text Hierarchy
- Hero headline: large, bold, emotional
- Section headlines: clear and short
- Supporting text: minimal

### Copy Rules
- Short sentences
- One idea per line
- Easy to scan
- No jargon

---

## 6. Graphics & Visual Elements

### Visual Style
- Sticker-like illustrations
- Expressive characters
- Thick outlines
- Bright emotions

### Key Visual Pattern
- **Before / After (Was → Became)**
- Real photo → stylized sticker
- Transformation must be visually obvious

---

## 7. Page Structure & UX

## 7.1 Hero Section (Above the Fold)

### Hero Headline (examples)
- **“Turn Any Photo into a Sticker”**
- **“Your Photos. Instantly Stickers.”**
- **“Create Stickers from Your Photos in Seconds”**

### Hero Subheadline (examples)
- “Upload a photo — get a ready-to-use Telegram sticker”
- “No editing. No skills. Just one click.”

### Hero Visual
- Sticker character with strong emotion
- Style representative of Lovable output

### Primary CTA Button
- **Text:**  
  - “Open in Telegram”  
  - “Create Stickers in Telegram”
- Telegram icon required
- Large, high-contrast
- Dominant visual element

---

## 7.2 Transformation Block (Key Focus Area)

This block is **mandatory** and visually dominant after the hero.

### Section Headline (examples)
- **“From Photo to Sticker”**
- **“Turn Your Photo into a Sticker”**
- **“Choose a Photo. Get a Sticker.”**

### Visual Structure
- Side-by-side or step-based layout:
  - **Left:** “Before” (original photo)
  - **Right:** “After” (Lovable sticker)
- Clear labels:
  - “Before”
  - “After”
- Directional arrow or transition effect

### Supporting Copy (optional)
- “Lovable keeps facial features and emotions”
- “Perfect for chats, reactions, and gifts”

---

## 7.3 Simple 2-Step Flow (Optional but Recommended)

### Step 1
**Title:** “Choose a Photo”  
**Description:** “Upload any portrait or photo”

### Step 2
**Title:** “Turn It into a Sticker”  
**Description:** “Lovable creates a ready-to-use sticker automatically”

Visual arrows or connectors are recommended.

---

## 8. Call-to-Action (CTA)

### Primary CTA Text (approved options)
- “Open in Telegram”
- “Create Stickers in Telegram”
- “Start in Telegram”

### CTA Rules
- Only one primary CTA
- No secondary buttons
- CTA repeated after transformation block
- Sticky CTA on mobile allowed

---

## 9. Mobile-First UX Principles

- CTA visible without scrolling
- Button height ≥ 48px
- Large tap zones
- Minimal scrolling
- Fast perceived load
- Thumb-friendly spacing

---

## 10. Conversion Optimization Principles

- Show the result before explaining anything
- Use emotion-driven visuals
- Reduce thinking to zero
- Make Telegram transition obvious and safe
- Avoid anything that looks like registration or setup

---

## 11. Summary

This landing page style works for Lovable because:
- It visually explains the product without text
- It matches Telegram’s expressive, casual culture
- It encourages impulsive, curiosity-driven clicks
- It removes fear around AI complexity

### Critical Elements (Must Not Be Removed)
- Before / After transformation block
- Single Telegram CTA
- Sticker-like emotional visuals
- Result-first communication
- Mobile-first layout
